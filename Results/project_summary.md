# Coffee Sales Analysis - Executive Summary

## Objective

Analyze coffee shop transaction data to identify sales trends, product performance and revenue drivers.

---

## Dataset Overview

- Transactions: 3,547
- Revenue: 112,245.58
- Products: 8
- Period: March 2024 - March 2025

---

## Key Findings

### Highest Revenue Product

Latte generated the highest revenue:

26,875.30

representing approximately 23.94% of total revenue.

---

### Most Purchased Product

Americano with Milk recorded:

809 transactions

making it the most frequently purchased product.

---

### Best Month

March generated:

15,891.64

the highest monthly revenue.

---

### Best Day

Tuesday generated:

18,168.38

the highest revenue among weekdays.

---

### Peak Hour

10 AM generated:

10,198.52

making it the strongest sales hour.

---

## Recommendations

1. Promote Latte and Americano with Milk.

2. Schedule more staff between:
   - 8 AM – 11 AM
   - 4 PM – 7 PM

3. Investigate the low performance of Espresso.

4. Replicate strategies used during high-performing months.
